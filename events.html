Mr. & Ms. VIBGYOR

It is the most highlighted event of the fest, where students compete for the title of Mr. & Ms. VIBGYOR, and they are judged on the basis of various parameters and they have to cross various rounds for the finale.

SOLO DANCE

A solo dance is danced by an individual dancing alone, as opposed to couples dancing together but independently of others dancing at the same time, if any, and as opposed to groups of people dancing simultaneously in a coordinated manner.


DUO DANCE

Here, the most concerned part of dance is coordination. The team which does that, rules the game.


GROUP DANCE

An act for representing a story behind the bars. Here thrilling and breath taking moves are shown to the live aufie.


STAND UP COMEDY

If you have the power of making someone laugh, then you are a creature similar to an angel. It is a platform given to the students to showcase their comic style eventually by speaking.


RANGOLI MAKING

It is an art form, originating in Indian Subcontinents in which patterns are created on the floor or on thr ground using colored rice, dry flour, colored sand.


BATTLE OF BANDS

Music is nothing without tunes. It is a contest in which you are the robber of the whole stage.


SOLO SINGING

Singing is a way of escaping. It's another world and you are no longer on earth. It is an act of producing musical sound with the voice and show your pure soul.


DUET SINGING

A duet is a musical composition for two performers in which the performers have equal importance to the piece, often a composition involving two singers.


QAWWALI

It is an energetic musical performance of poetry that aims to lead listeners to a state of calm.


Photography Competition

Taking an image, freezing a moment, reveals how rich reality truly is, In photography there is a reality so subtle that it becomes more real than reality.


fashion show

It is a huge event where all the departments of college compete and showcase their fashion ethics! And the best one steals the moment!


skit

It is a short informal performance intended to educate or inform. Here various groups compete together and show their acting skills on the stage.


nukkad natak

Street theatre or Nukkad Natak is a form of theatrical performance and presentation in outdoor public spaces without a specific paying audience. These spaces can be anywhere.


Film making

Filmmaking is the process of making a film, generally in the sense of films intended for extensive theatrical exhibition.
